NeraEngHub

Our specialized engineering and management programs offer an opportunity to explore corrosion prevention, project, product & and product management, and climate action planning through in-person and virtual seminars on: cathodic protection design, installation, maintenance, coatings inspection and testing, and reporting; Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) foundations, materiality & strategy, management & execution, disclosure & communication as well as exposure to Clean Energy Transition: carbon capture utilization and storage (CCUS), clean hydrogen and ammonia, solar power generation and storage, offshore wind applications and design, electric vehicles and the impact on existing grids.

NeraStyleHub

Our Style hub provides an opportunity to develop skills in appreciation and correction of apparel fit, application of ideal fit standards across different body types and fabrics, pattern drafting and sewing skills, use of specialty industrial sewing and pressing machines, essentials of technical illustration, pattern making, pattern grading and clothing construction, manufacturing inputs and processes and movement in the supply chain, as well as digital marketing and management of the apparel value chain.

NeraTechHub

This program is designed to provide you with in-depth knowledge on how to create websites and ensure user accessibility by implementing web designs through the use of various programming languages, write logic for software programs, build API and database connectivity, use third party services, and provide the infrastructure for software applications to work effectively: program servers, client-side interfaces, and design databases using various programming languages as well as building responsive mobile apps for both Android and iOS operating systems and different screen sizes.
